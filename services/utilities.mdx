# Utility Services

<Icon icon="bolt" size={32} /> **Essential Services for Your Home and Business**

The City of Oak Point provides reliable, affordable utility services to all residents and businesses within the city limits. Our modern infrastructure and responsive customer service ensure you have the services you need when you need them.

### Service Connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Service Application">
    Complete application online or visit Customer Service
  </Step>
  <Step title="Property Inspection">
    We verify meter location and service requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connection Installation">
    Professional installation typically within 3-5 business days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Service Activation">
    Your service is activated and billing begins
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Connection Fees:**
- Residential: $350
- Commercial: $500-$1,200 (based on meter size)

### Billing Cycle

<Note>
Bills are mailed on the **1st of each month** and are due by the **25th**. Late fees apply after the due date.
</Note>

| Service | Residential Rate | Commercial Rate |
|---------|------------------|-----------------|
| Water (per 1,000 gallons) | $3.25 | $4.15 |
| Sewer (per 1,000 gallons) | $4.10 | $5.25 |
| Base Service Fee | $18.50/month | $35.00/month |
| Trash Collection | $22.00/month | $45.00/month |
