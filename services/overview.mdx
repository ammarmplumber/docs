## How to Access Services

<Steps>
  <Step title="Online Portal" icon="computer">
    Most services are available 24/7 through our online portal at **portal.oakpoint.gov**
  </Step>
  <Step title="By Phone" icon="phone">
    Call our main line at **(555) 123-4567** during business hours: **Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM**
  </Step>
  <Step title="In Person" icon="building">
    Visit **City Hall** at **100 Oak Point Plaza** - convenient parking available
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mobile App" icon="mobile">
    Download the **Oak Point Connect** app for service requests and notifications
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Service Standards

<Note>
**Our Promise**: We strive to respond to all service requests within **24 hours** and resolve issues as quickly as possible.
</Note>

| Service Type | Response Time | Processing Time |
|--------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Emergency Services | Immediate | Immediate |
| Utility Issues | 2-4 hours | Same day |
| Permit Applications | 1 business day | 3-10 business days |
| General Inquiries | 24 hours | 48 hours |

## Popular Services

<Accordion title="Utility Services" icon="bolt">
  - **Water & Sewer**: Service connections, billing, and emergency repairs
  - **Trash & Recycling**: Collection schedules and special pickup requests  
  - **Storm Water**: Drainage issues and flood prevention
  
  <Tip>Set up auto-pay for utilities to never miss a payment!</Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Development Services" icon="hammer">
  - **Building Permits**: Residential and commercial construction
  - **Zoning**: Land use and development regulations
  - **Inspections**: Required inspections for construction projects
  
  <Info>Most permits can be applied for online with same-day review!</Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Public Works" icon="road">
  - **Street Maintenance**: Repairs, resurfacing, and snow removal
  - **Traffic Management**: Signals, signs, and parking enforcement
  - **Parks & Facilities**: Maintenance of public spaces
</Accordion>
