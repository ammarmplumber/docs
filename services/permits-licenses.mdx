# Permits & Licenses

<Icon icon="file-certificate" size={32} /> **Making the Process Simple and Transparent**

Whether you're planning a home renovation, starting a business, or organizing an event, we've streamlined our permit and licensing process to get you what you need quickly and efficiently.

## Online Application Portal

<Card title="Apply Online 24/7" icon="laptop" href="https://permits.oakpoint.gov">
  Submit applications, track status, schedule inspections, and pay fees online
</Card>

<Info>
**Processing Time**: Most permits are reviewed within **3-5 business days**. Complex commercial projects may take up to **10 business days**.
</Info>

## Building Permits

<Accordion title="Residential Permits" icon="house">
  
  ### What Requires a Permit?
  
  <Check>**Requires Permit**</Check>
  - New construction or additions
  - Structural modifications
  - Electrical, plumbing, or HVAC work
  - Decks, patios, and driveways
  - Fences over 6 feet tall
  - Swimming pools and spas
  
  <Warning>**No Permit Needed**</Warning>
  - Interior painting or flooring
  - Landscaping (non-structural)
  - Minor repairs and maintenance
  - Fences under 6 feet
  
  ### Application Process
  
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Submit Plans">
      Upload detailed drawings and project specifications
    </Step>
    <Step title="Plan Review">
      Our team reviews for code compliance (3-5 business days)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Permit Issued">
      Pay fees and receive permit number
    </Step>
    <Step title="Schedule Inspections">
      Book required inspections through our online system
    </Step>
  </Steps>

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Commercial Permits" icon="building">
  
  Commercial projects require additional documentation and may involve multiple departments.
  
  **Required Documents:**
  - Architectural plans (sealed by licensed architect)
  - Engineering drawings (if applicable)
  - Environmental impact assessment
  - Parking and traffic study
  - Fire safety plan
  
  <Tip>
  Schedule a **pre-application meeting** for complex projects. It's free and can save weeks in the approval process!
  </Tip>

</Accordion>

## Business Licenses

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="General Business License" icon="briefcase">
    Required for most businesses operating within city limits
    
    **Fee**: $125/year  
    **Processing**: 1-2 business days
  </Card>
  <Card title="Home Business License" icon="house-laptop">
    Special license for businesses operated from residential properties
    
    **Fee**: $75/year  
    **Processing**: 1-2 business days
  </Card>
  <Card title="Food Service License" icon="utensils">
    Required for restaurants, food trucks, and catering
    
    **Fee**: $200/year  
    **Processing**: 5-7 business days
  </Card>
  <Card title="Liquor License" icon="wine-glass">
    Various types available for different establishments
    
    **Fee**: $500-$2,000/year  
    **Processing**: 15-30 business days
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
