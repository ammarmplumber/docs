# Welcome to the City of Oak Point

**Where Community Grows Strong** <Icon icon="seedling" />

The City of Oak Point is committed to providing exceptional services while preserving our small-town charm and fostering sustainable growth. Our dedicated team works every day to ensure our residents, businesses, and visitors experience the very best our community has to offer.
